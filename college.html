Nirmala College of Arts and Science was established in 2014 to cater to the ever-changing requirements of the student community for undergraduate programmes. The college is affiliated with the University of Calicut and offers MCom, BCA, BBA, B.Com (Computer Application, Cooperation, Finance), BA Multimedia, B.Sc. Computer Science, B.Sc. Costume & Fashion Designing, BA English, BTTM (Bachelor of Travel and Tourism Management), BTHM (Bachelor of Tourism & Hotel Management) and B.Sc. Food Technology programmes. The college is located at an eco-friendly campus in Kunnappilly, Meloor is equipped with modern infrastructural facilities that enhance the ambience for learning and personal development. As an institution committed to hard work and perfection, Nirmala endeavours to bring out the best in every student by imparting a strong educational foundation by offering exceptional student experiences, especially in the context of the emerging challenges of the 21st century.
